Golfmann is a technology-driven financial services and investment management firm that offers a full suite of financial solutions across the digital assets ecosystem to direct clients and institutions.

GFMNs are tokenised shares of the US based investment firm Golfmann LLC and have a finite supply of 1,000,000 unique tokens.

GFMNs investors are Golfmann LLC’s shareholders by default. The shareholders are listed as the proprietors of the Golfmann trading system. A brand-new trading system based on Artificial Intelligence.

GFMN tokens are purchased by the investor. The minimum purchase is 1,000 tokens, or 0.1% of Golfmann LLC's shares. At the time of purchase, the firm had a total capitalisation of $1,000,000. At the end of the fiscal year, with an apparent net income of $400,000, the company's capitalisation is $1,400,000. An investor’s return will be 40% of their initial invested capital. (All taxes are paid in full by the client.)

At the conclusion of each fiscal year, shareholders will be notified of four withdrawal options:

-Reinvesting the entire capital for the next fiscal year

-Reinvesting the initial capital and taking profits

-Reinvesting profits and withdrawing the initial capital

-Selling GFMN tokens back to Golfmann LLC and withdrawing the whole amount. (Selling GFMNs to anyone else without notifying the board members may result in a permanent loss of your funds.)
